[app.basalted.com](https://app.basalted.com) — Pro tier.

## Sign up

Sign in with GitHub or Google. We don't ask for a password and we don't store
your provider's auth token beyond the session.

## Register a vault

- Upload a `.zip` of your vault, or
- Connect Vault Sync (E2EE) from the desktop app

## Generate a brief

The cockpit runs the same engine as the CLI, hosted on Cloudflare Workers.
Latency: p95 < 8 s on a 1k-note vault.

## Findings history

Every finding from every brief is browsable on the timeline. Pin findings,
mark them as resolved, or promote them back to your vault.

## Billing

Manage subscription, view invoices, and cancel in **Settings → Billing**.
We use Stripe; we never see your card.
