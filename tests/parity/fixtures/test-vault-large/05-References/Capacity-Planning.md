What I keep noticing in my own work is compounding only happens to people who don't optimize for the next quarter. If you take the long view, the moat isn't speed alone — it's the user's willingness to keep coming back.

Related: [[Beacon-Note-02]], [[Greta]], [[Beacon-Note-10]].

The thing that keeps coming up is you can't fix a culture problem with tooling, but the wrong tooling rots a healthy one.
