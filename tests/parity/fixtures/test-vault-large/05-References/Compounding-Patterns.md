The bit I keep forgetting and re-deriving is the cheapest correction happens when the constraint is still legible. What I keep noticing in my own work is I keep being wrong about how long the patient version takes — usually longer than I think, and worth it. What I keep noticing in my own work is the index is the product. The interface is editorial. On reflection, the opposite is closer to true.

Related: [[Sapolsky-2004]], [[Beacon-Note-02]], [[Atlas-Note-14]].

What I keep noticing in my own work is what you measure is what you incentivize. What you publicize is what you actually value. What I keep noticing in my own work is the right abstraction is the one you can throw away in six months without panic. I've changed my mind on this; the failure mode is more common than the success mode.
