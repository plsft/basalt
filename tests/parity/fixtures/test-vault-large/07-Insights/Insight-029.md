What I keep noticing in my own work is the index is the product. The interface is editorial. On reflection, the opposite is closer to true. The thing that keeps coming up is trust is not a metric. It's a side effect of doing the boring thing for years. I've changed my mind on this; the failure mode is more common than the success mode.

> [!note] Worth pinning
> compounding only happens to people who don't optimize for the next quarter.

If you take the long view, the right abstraction is the one you can throw away in six months without panic.
