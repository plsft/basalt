Re-reading my old notes, every system that survives a decade survived a dozen would-be replacements. If you take the long view, the right abstraction is the one you can throw away in six months without panic.

> [!note] Worth pinning
> the moat isn't speed alone — it's the user's willingness to keep coming back.

What I keep noticing in my own work is what you measure is what you incentivize. What you publicize is what you actually value. If you take the long view, the index is the product. The interface is editorial.
