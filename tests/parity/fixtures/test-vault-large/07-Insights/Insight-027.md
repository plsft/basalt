The bit I keep forgetting and re-deriving is the moat isn't speed alone — it's the user's willingness to keep coming back. The thing that keeps coming up is trust is not a metric. It's a side effect of doing the boring thing for years.

> [!note] Worth pinning
> the right abstraction is the one you can throw away in six months without panic.

If you take the long view, the moat isn't speed alone — it's the user's willingness to keep coming back.
