What I keep noticing in my own work is trust is not a metric. It's a side effect of doing the boring thing for years. Re-reading my old notes, what you measure is what you incentivize. What you publicize is what you actually value. On reflection, the opposite is closer to true. The thing that keeps coming up is the moat isn't speed alone — it's the user's willingness to keep coming back. I've changed my mind on this; the failure mode is more common than the success mode.
