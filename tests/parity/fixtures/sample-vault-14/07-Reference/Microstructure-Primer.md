# Microstructure Primer

> Working notes on the microstructure literature relevant to [[HYPOTHESIS]].

## Order-flow imbalance (OFI)

Defined as signed volume per millisecond. Hasbrouck (2007) showed it explains
~30% of intraday return variance in equity. The crypto-perp analog is
unstudied at sub-second granularity — gap.

## VPIN (Easley, López de Prado, O'Hara, 2012)

Volume-bucketed toxicity proxy. Original paper claims predictive power in
flash-crash regimes. Replication record is mixed.

## Open questions

- Does OFI generalize across CEXes when liquidity is fragmented?
- Is VPIN a directional signal or only a regime-detection tool?
- What's the right half-life?
