# SignalBot — Original Hypothesis

> **One-line:** Microstructure signals are the only retail-accessible alpha that survives multiple-testing correction.

## Context

Six months of literature review and personal backtests have converged on a single conviction. Most "alpha" signals collapse under Bonferroni; the few that don't share one property — they encode order-flow microstructure, not price.

## The claim

> [!warning] Diminishing Returns vs Structural Edge
> Pure-latency strategies on retail venues yield ~1% per cycle now, down from 2-3% a year ago. The market has caught up with speed. However:
>
> - **Order-flow imbalance** is invariant to latency
> - **Volume-weighted toxicity (VPIN)** survives walk-forward in 4 of 6 venues tested
> - **Liquidation-cascade prediction** is unique to crypto perps and not commoditized
>
> The sustainable edge isn't speed alone — it's speed + intelligence.

## What this means

If the claim holds under proper out-of-sample validation, it implies the next 12 months of research should center on microstructure signal *combinations*, not on latency reduction or new venue discovery.
